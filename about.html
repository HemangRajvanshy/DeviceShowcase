Operation

The machine is operated in a manner similar to a typical exercise bike; the user will sit on the seat and rotate the pedals using their legs. As the pedals are rotated, they spin an axle that is connected to a system of gears, progressively increasing the RPM generated by the user by a significant amount. The system outputs its rotational motion to a belt that is connected to an electric dynamo. We use a Shimano Alfine dynamo to convert the energy from pedaling into electric energy, thus the dynamo is highly efficient and requires virtually no maintenance.

The Electricycle houses a WEN mini-generator battery that can store up to 2 kilowatt-hours energy which is just enough to store enough energy to power your home while being small enough to fit within the product's dimensions. The battery has a power output of 1000 watts, meaning that it can power forty 25-Watt light bulbs at once. The output from the Electricycle can be plugged directly into any device as it is very similar to the energy output from the U.S. power grid. On top of all of this, a wide, high-quality biking seat is attached to ensure comfort for long durations of usage. The make-up of our device creates a durable, long-lasting, and effective generator that has many different uses.

The Electricycle can also pair with your mobile device via Bluetooth. Once paired, The Electricycle App can be used to control the bike, check the status of your battery, and track your workouts. The app can also provide these metrics over time and lets you track your workouts.

Follow The Energy

Since the primary purpose of designing this device is to reveal its energy inputs, it is important to also understand the transfer of energy that has to occur for this machine to operate and be functional. Let’s start following the path of the energy within this system when it is still in the form of calories in food. The chemical bonds within food are broken, converting the substances into more useful forms of energy for humans through a complex digestion process. One of the main purposes of digestion is to provide your cells with the necessary energy to operate through a molecular chemical called ATP. Humans can utilize this energy to partake in many activities; however, within our product, we attempt to harness human's ability to use their muscles to produce rotational motion by pushing pedals. The rotation of the Electricycle’s pedals will then turn a dynamo, producing electricity that may be stored, used, or transported elsewhere.

Some of this electric energy will be used to power the Bluetooth module that enables communication with The Electricycle App, while the rest will be available for use through outlets on the bike’s rear. Unused energy is simply stored in the WEN mini-generator battery until it needed to power other devices at a later time. By understanding the bike's energy flow, we are better informed about the complex role energy truly plays in our lives.

Influencing Behavior

One of the main aspirations we had while designing our product was to change the user’s perspective on energy and its influence on the world. People often interact with devices that obscure their energy inputs because their designers usually found it unnecessary to display these inputs, thus leading individuals to rarely question where their energy is even coming from. However, our product was developed with that goal of putting a strong emphasis on its energy inputs and conveying the importance of energy to its user. Multiple studies support our belief that cognitive and emotional behaviors are associated with physical actions. The action of performing some task can often lead an individual to either reinforce or alter, but unquestionably influence, their views concerning that task. With this idea in mind, the Electricycle was designed to change the user’s perspective towards energy by pushing them to realize how their labor directly relates to energy production fueling their device.

As you pedal the Electricycle with unwavering determination, you can look at your daily workout statistics to better understand how your efforts equate to the energy consumption of several household products. By reviewing The Electricycle app, you will gradually begin internalizing the amount of energy you use every day. This product urges you to think about ways you can cut your power consumption all while the app frequently displays energy saving tips at the beginning of every session.
