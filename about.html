Welcome

Operation

The machine is operated in a manner similar to a typical exercise bike; the user will sit on the seat and rotate the pedals using their legs. As the pedals are rotated, they spin an axle that is connected to a system of gears, progressively increasing the RPM generated by the user by a significant amount. The system outputs its rotational motion to a belt that is connected to an electric dynamo. We use a Shimano Alfine dynamo to convert the energy from pedaling into electric energy, the dynamo is highly efficient and requires virtually no maintenance.

The Electricycle houses a WEN mini-generator battery that can store upto 2 kilowatt-hours energy, just enough to store enough energy to power your home while being small enough to fit with product dimensions. The battery has a power output of 1000 watts, meaning that it can power forty 25-Watt light bulbs at once. The output from The Electricycle can be plugged directly into any device, as it is very similar to the energy output from the grid. On top of all of this, a wide, high-quality biking seat will be attached to ensure comfort for long durations of usage. The make-up of our device creates a durable, long-lasting, and effective generator that has many different uses.

The Electricycle can pair with your mobile device via Bluetooth. Once paired, The Electricycle App can be used to control the bike, check the status of your battery, and track your workouts. . The app can also provide these metrics over time, and lets you track your workouts.

Follow The Energy

Since the primary purpose of designing this device is revealing its energy inputs, it is important to also understand the transfer of energy that has to occur for this machine to operate and be functional. Let’s start following the path of the energy within this system when it is still in the form of calories in food. The chemical bonds within food are broken, converting the substances into more useful forms of energy for humans through a complex digestion process. One of the main purposes of digestion is to provide your cells with the necessary energy to operate through a chemical structure called ATP . Humans can utilize this energy to partake in many activities; however, within our machine, we attempt to harness humans’ ability to use their muscles to produce rotational motion by pushing pedals. The rotation of the Electricycle’s pedals will then turn a dynamo, producing electricity that may be stored, used, or transported elsewhere.

Some of this electric energy will be used to power the bluetooth device that enables communication with The Electricycle App, while the rest will be available for use through outlets on the bike’s rear. Unused energy is stored in the WEN mini-generator battery and can be used anytime. The bike fails to produce even a tiny percentage of your home's energy needs. Nevertheless, it serves to inform us of the complex role energy plays in our lives.
