Device Description

Even though this product draws inspiration from a modern stationary exercise bike, we have sought to improve upon its design in order to bring greater utility to your home. One of the main functionalities of our device is that it allows you to generate power while pedaling, thus allowing to improve your health while powering your gadgets. A highly efficient electrical dynamo located within the bike converts your hardwork to electricity that can either be stored in an internal battery or outputed through a rear outlet. The Electricycle comes with its own mobile app. You can use the app to track your workouts, see how many calories your burned in each session and how much electricity you generated. The app also has a live mode that you can activate during your workout in order to track your power output. The app presents you with information about how much energy the internal battery has, and for how long you can power common household devices with the energy stored in the battery.

In order to allow the bike to be used in a wide variety of locations in your home, the handle bars normally found on exercise bikes have been removed to allow you to pedal both in the gym as well as at a desk. This allows exercise and work to be combined together and facilitates productivity.

By serving both as a generator and an exercise machine, The Electricycle will gradually make you aware of the influence electricity has on your life while helping you learn how your labor equates to the energy consumption of modern devices. By highlighting how energy moves and is transformed within our world, this device will develop your understanding of the true scale of your home's electricity usage.

Real World Applications

Our product's ability to generate electricity independent of the presence of a power grid, makes the Electricyle extremely beneficial in regions affected by natural disasters or power outages. Although labor is involved in the power generation process, individuals with no alternative sources of electricity could potentially use pedaling as a way to power devices. Individuals in a power outage could power their gadgets through pedaling without the need to purchase an expensive generator. A family could quickly create enough energy to power the necessary devices to survive inclement weather, get information from the news or stay warm through artificial heating (and exercising).

Additionally, third-world countries could greatly benefit from our product. Those who are in need of energy can have it after a short period of pedaling, without the need for natural resources to power our generator. This results in a lack of additional costs typically associated with other forms of energy production, and it is sustainable enough to take care of everyday needs. Yet another application is the generation of power during a blackout (caused by severe weather). One could quickly create enough energy to power the necessary devices to survive inclement weather, be it getting information from the news or staying warm with your central heating system.
